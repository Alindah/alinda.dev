Hi, I'm Alinda! So you wanna know about me, eh?

Born and raised in Los Angeles, transplant to San Francisco in 2019. I graduated from CSU Channel Islands in 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science, with a sprinkle of Mathematics and a dash of Robotics Engineering.

"What do you want to be when you grow up?" has always been a challenging question to me. My education points me to tech; however, my interests aren't limited to practical pursuits. In fact, my career ambitions began on the creative side. I wanted to be a painter, an architect, a tattoo artist... and then later a surgeon, a video game designer, and a paramedic.

I realized I couldn't just pick one thing when there are so many interesting roles life has to offer! My dream is to become a Renaissance woman (otherwise known as a polymath) — someone who excels in many disciplines. Hey, this makes me an excellent generalist!

I eventually discovered that programming is a good balance between left brain and right brain; it's a practical skill that allows me to create.

And boy do I love creating. I mainly satisfy my thirst for it by making digital art. More recently, I learned how to make my own music using a DAW. I hope to make my very own bona fide video games some day.

My other hobbies involve adventuring through the digital world of video games (FPS and RPGs are my favorite!) and playing guitar. I might also be pretending to be a little boy in a D&D campaign. Otherwise, you can find me in the kitchen where I cook really bland foods (but let's not talk about that).

Anyway, I pay $12 / year for this site and have poured hours into it, so I hope you enjoy your stay here!
