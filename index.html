Hi, I'm Alinda! So you wanna know about me, eh?

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, but am now living in San Francisco. I graduated from CSU Channel Islands in 2017 with a BS in Computer Science (and less importantly, a minor in Mathematics and a minor in Robotics Engineering.)

My interests aren't limited to practical pursuits. In fact, my career ambitions began on the creative side. As a child, "what do you want to be when you grow up?" had always been a challenging question. I wanted to be a painter, an architect, a tattoo artist, a surgeon, a video game designer... I realized I couldn't just pick one thing; there are just too many interesting things in life! My dream is to become a Renaissance woman (otherwise known as a polymath) — someone who excels in many disciplines. On the bright side, this makes me an excellent generalist.

I eventually discovered that programming is a good balance between the two; it's a practical skill that allows me to create.

And boy do I love creating things. I mainly satisfy my thirst for it by making digital art. More recently, I learned how to make my own music using a DAW. I hope to make my own video games some day.

My other hobbies involve playing video games (FPS and RPGs are my favorite!) and playing guitar. I might also be pretending to be a little boy in a D&D campaign. I also like to cook really bland foods (but let's not talk about that.)

Anyway, I pay $12 / year for this site and have poured hours into it, so I hope you enjoy your stay here!
